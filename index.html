Gachiakuta

TV Series Seasons: 1 Episodes: 24 Action,Fantasy,shonen 2025

Rudo lives at the bottom of a floating city, where the poor hide in the shadows of the palaces of the rich who flutter in a luxurious life. They have no problem throwing their waste in a desolate place called “the abyss”. In the midst of this misery, Rudo is framed for a murder he did not commit, and is unjustly convicted and thrown into the abyss along with all other human waste. But that abyss was not only a dumping ground for waste, but also a breeding ground for foul and ferocious monsters born of human filth. If Rudo wants to extract the truth from the darkness of this hell, and take revenge on those who threw him into it, he must tame the mysterious power hidden in his gloves, and make it a weapon with which to make his way between shadows and monsters.
